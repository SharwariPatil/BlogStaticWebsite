About us

1. Who we are?

We are a [Y Combinator](https://ycombinator.com) backed startup solving the employability problem in India. We run a 6-month program for college students/ graduates to upskill themselves and break into software dev roles at top tech companies. We work on an outcome-based income sharing model and our students pay us only after getting placed with a salary greater than 5 LPA. We have been able to help students from Tier-2/3 colleges get into companies like Amazon, Paytm, Park+, etc., and have been able to see a huge impact on student's learnings and careers. Currently, we are training 1500+ students, helping them become Full Stack Developers. We are looking for passionate folks to help us scale this :)
